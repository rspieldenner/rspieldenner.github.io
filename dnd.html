Lich's Lackeys

Outline

In progress work on a potential campaign with divergence for heroic and villianous parties

Based in Waterdeep. Patron a handler for one of the masked lords.

  1. Start out running missions for a patron
    • Clean out nest of goblins
    • Protect caravans from bandits
    • Root out a protection racket
    • Find some items in Undermountain
  2. Morally questionable quests
    • Wipe out a tribe of barbarians
    • Attack a goblinoid army led by hobgoblins - find out they were hired by patron and then betrayed him/her.
    • Collect money from farmers/villagers/shopkeepers/guild
  3. Decision Point: You know your patron is up to no good do you embrace the path towards darkness, or fight against them?
  4. Trip to Sigil
  5. Trip to City of Brass
  6. Tu'noroth
